mission for successs

Because every child is capable and deserving of reaching her or his full potential. Our mission is to provide tools and resources that empowers both educators and children to experience learning through creative expression, RSDR empowers the teachers to instil the correct kind of learning among the young minds. With a thematic and integrated learning approach honed with the best pedagogies for the young ones, RSDR helps pre-schoolers assimilate different values and life skills that are imperative for their holistic development.
